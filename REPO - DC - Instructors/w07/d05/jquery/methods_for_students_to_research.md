## Common jQuery Methods

You just learned how to create jQuery objects. Now head into your sandbox and make use of the following methods. You'll have to look some of them up at MDN.

* `.text()`
* `.html()`
* `.attr()`
* `.addClass()`
* `.removeClass()`
* `.toggleClass()`
* `.css()`
* `.remove()`
* `.append()`
* `.prepend()`
